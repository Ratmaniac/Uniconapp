About Us

back

UniCon

(short for Universal Convention) is a festival that is going to take place in Riga, on 3 and 4 August 2013 for the first time.

The goal of this festival is to unite and collect people of any ages, hobbies, and interests in one place for a fascinating pastime, and, perhaps,

introduce them to something new and interesting.
